## Important Limitations

This data captures national averages and does NOT account for:
- **Location**: SF Bay Area salaries 40-50% higher than rural areas
- **Company size**: Fortune 500 vs startup 30-100% difference
- **Specialization**: Sub-specialties command 15-50% premium
- **Gender/Race**: Systemic wage gaps exist (10-25% differences)
- **Experience**: Varies year by year (data shows 5-year snapshots)
- **Market cycles**: Recessions and booms affect significantly
- **Benefits**: Pension value, healthcare, 401k not calculated
- **Future automation**: AI impact on job demand unclear
